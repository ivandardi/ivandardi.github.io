About Ivan Dardi

I am Ivan Dardi, a software engineer living in São Paulo, Brazil.

  • Background
  • Projects
  • Experiences

Background

I have two degrees in Computer Science from the Federal University of São Paulo (UNIFESP). I have been working as a software engineer ever since 2019. I've never had an internship during my college years, so my first job was as a Salesforce Consultant. I worked as one for 3 months before getting yoinked into a software engineering and DevOps position at a fintech company. That was the start of the finances part of my career.

Over the course of the years, I jumped between finances companies. Digital banks, financial advice platforms, and day trading platforms. Then I decided I really don't like working in finance, so I moved to a company that works with modern mobility solutions. I'm currently working there happily using Rust, which has been a long term career goal of mine.

Projects

I currently run the Rust Programming Language Discord Server on Discord, which as of 2024-04-28 has around 52k members. It was founded in 2017 by me during my college years as a way to learn more about the language and meet new people. It has since grown into a community of people who are passionate about Rust and want to help others learn it.

To manage that server, we use a bot called FerrisBot for Discord. It's a collaborative open source project between me and the members of the server.

Experiences

I have experience with a wide range of technologies, including:

  1. Software development (Rust, Python)
  2. Linux (Bash)
  3. DevOps (AWS, Kubernetes, Docker, Terraform)
  4. CI Tools (Jenkins, GitHub Actions, GoCD)
  5. Databases (SQLite and Postgres my beloveds)
  6. Version Control Systems (git, GitHub, Bitbucket)
  7. Frontend development (Javascript, HTML, CSS, the usual)

I also used to work with OpenCV doing computer vision for college projects, but I haven't touched it in a while.

I like playing with Virtual Reality (VR) stuff on the side. It's a mix of Blender, Unity, Substance Painter and Photoshop alternatives. I'm not very good at it, but I enjoy it a lot!
